MAJOR SERVER RULES

Permanent Ban & Character Wipe Offenses

ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY

Violation of any major rule will result in immediate Permanent Ban and possible character wipe

GENERAL OFFENSES

Abuse & Harassment

Permanent Ban

Any form of abuse, taunting, or harassment will result in permanent ban.

  • Disrespecting or abusing staff members
  • Disrespect to content creators or female players (no second chances)
  • Abusive behavior in any form

Ban Evasion

Permanent Ban

Attempting to bypass bans or restrictions will lead to further penalties.

  • Using alternate accounts to join gang VCs when banned
  • Playing from other Steam accounts
  • Gangs allowing banned members in VC will be deleted

Real Money Transactions

Permanent Ban

Selling in-game assets for real money is strictly prohibited.

  • Selling in-game cash, cars, or any items
  • Real money trading of any kind

BUG ABUSE & EXPLOITS

Game Exploits

Permanent Ban

Exploiting game mechanics or bugs for unfair advantage.

  • Arena bug to increase health/armor
  • Spraying at territories while riding vehicles
  • F8 quit command exploitation
  • Canceling server-side animations
  • Any major/minor bug abuse including duping guns/ammo

Unauthorized Modifications

Permanent Ban

Using prohibited modifications that provide unfair advantages.

  • Graphic packs that remove water, bushes or props
  • Any modification that gives competitive advantage
  • Unauthorized recording methods (only NVIDIA/AMD allowed)

Weapon Boxes

Permanent Ban

Unauthorized possession of weapon boxes is strictly prohibited.

  • Only whitelisted factions may carry weapon boxes
  • Any unauthorized possession will result in ban

ROLEPLAY & GAMEPLAY

Character Wipe Offenses

Permanent Ban

Serious violations that result in complete character removal.

  • Sentenced to death by Chief Justice (character removed)
  • Roleplaying same character after death sentence
  • Major bug abuse may result in complete account wipe

Gang Violations

Permanent Ban

Gang-related offenses with severe consequences.

  • If 3+ gang members caught exploiting spray bug, whole gang banned
  • Dragging permanently banned players into gang VC will delete waiting VC

Property & Economy

Permanent Ban

Abusing property or economic systems.

  • Storing white money in house inventories
  • Entering labs without required cards
  • Any job/robbery/drug related major bug abuse

STAFF & SECURITY

Privacy Violations

Permanent Ban

Unauthorized access to player information or systems.

  • Checking another player's PC without staff authorization
  • Violations result in ban from both Discord and game

SERVER INTEGRITY

These rules exist to maintain fair play and quality roleplay for all members. Permanent bans are irreversible.
