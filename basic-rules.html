Essential Guidelines

These fundamental rules ensure fair play, immersive roleplay, and a respectful environment for all community members. Violations of these rules will result in immediate disciplinary action.

GENERAL ROLEPLAY RULES

Core principles that govern all roleplay interactions and maintain server quality.

Event Disturbing

Intentionally disrupting or interfering with server events, community gatherings, or organized activities.

Example: Shooting during a peaceful car meet, interrupting RP events, or causing chaos during organized activities.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Cop Baiting

Provoking law enforcement without proper RP reason or using derogatory terms towards PD/EMS.

Example: Saying "thulla" to PD/EMS, intentionally taunting officers, or creating situations solely to provoke police response.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Disrespecting Body (18+)

Using inappropriate emotes or actions with bodies, including sexual or degrading behaviors.

Example: Peeing/pooping on bodies, other 18+ emotes with corpses, or any form of body desecration.

Ban Duration: 48 Hours

Fail Roleplay

Actions that break immersion, don't make logical sense, or go against realistic character behavior.

Example: Jumping off buildings without reason, unrealistic actions, or breaking character immersion.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Erotic Roleplay (18+)

Engaging in sexual or explicit roleplay in public areas or without proper consent.

Example: Using sexual emotes or text RP in public areas, explicit content visible to other players.

Ban Duration: 72 Hours

Vehicle Death Match (VDM)

Using vehicles as weapons without proper RP initiation or realistic reasoning.

Example: Intentionally running over players without reason, ramming vehicles without RP context.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Fail Initiation

Starting conflicts without proper warning, counting, or realistic escalation.

Example: Shooting without counting properly, giving inadequate warning, or rushing into combat.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Intentional Pitting

Deliberately ramming vehicles to spin them out without proper RP justification.

Example: Side-swiping cars during chases without RP reason, aggressive ramming tactics.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

LIFE & DEATH RULES

Rules governing character life, death, and memory to maintain realistic roleplay continuity.

New Life Rule (NLR)

Cannot remember or return to previous life situations after respawning.

Example: Returning to a shootout location within 30 minutes of death, remembering who killed you.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

No Value of Life (NVL)

Acting without regard for your character's survival or safety.

Example: Jumping from moving vehicles, suicidal actions, ignoring threats to your life.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Revenge Roleplay

Seeking revenge for past situations your character shouldn't remember.

Example: Attacking someone because they robbed you in previous life, holding grudges after NLR.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

COMBAT RULES

Regulations governing combat situations, conflicts, and aggressive interactions.

Combat Reviving

Reviving or asking for revive during active combat situations.

Example: Getting revived while in shootout, asking for medical help during active combat.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Combat Logging

Leaving the game during active situations to avoid consequences.

Example: Disconnecting during police chase, logging out during robbery or combat.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Killing without proper RP reason, initiation, or realistic escalation.

Example: Shooting random players without cause, killing without proper RP buildup.

Ban Duration: 48 Hours

Combat Storing

Storing items, weapons, or evidence during active situations.

Example: Putting weapons in trunk during police chase, storing evidence during investigation.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

TECHNICAL RULES

Technical requirements and restrictions to ensure fair gameplay and server stability.

Meta Gaming

Using out-of-game information in roleplay situations.

Example: Checking player IDs, using Discord/YouTube info, stream sniping.

Ban Duration: 48 Hours

High Packet Loss

Playing with unstable connection during important situations.

Example: High ping/packet loss during Code Red, events, or critical RP situations.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

No Point of View (POV)

Failing to provide recording when requested by staff for rule violations.

Example: Unable to provide footage when reported, not recording during important situations.

Ban Duration: 24 Hours

Important Notice

All players are expected to read and understand these basic rules before joining the server. These rules form the foundation of our community standards. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for violations. Staff members have the discretion to interpret situations and apply appropriate punishments.
