Turf Zone Rules

Regulations specific to turf zone engagements, territorial conflicts, and gang warfare within designated combat areas.

1

Zone Boundary Compliance

Stay within the boundary of zone at all times during active conflicts.

2

No Re-entry Policy

If someone enters the boundary of zone, they can't leave & enter back again - you must fight once you enter the zone.

3

New Life Rule Application

The New Life Rule (NLR) applies across all zones without exception.

4

EMS Restriction

EMS cannot enter during zone fights under any circumstances.

5

Gang-Only Backup

Bring backup from your own gang, but no support from other gangs or alliances.

6

Anonymous Tweet Requirement

Before starting a code red in the zone, each gang needs to post an anonymous tweet.

7

Combat Reviving Prohibition

Combat reviving during active zone fights is strictly prohibited; both the revived and the reviver will face consequences.

Severe Penalties Apply
8

Timer End Compliance

Once the zone war timer ends, code red activities must cease in that area.

9

General Rules Application

All other server rules apply to these territory zones.

10

OOC "Over" Declaration

Once the zone war timer ends, every gang involved needs to type "over" in the out-of-character (OOC) chat.

11

Alliance Prohibition

Alliance is strictly not allowed at zones (even after the codered is over).

12

Safe Zone Proximity

Zone wars near the LSPD station and safe zones are strictly prohibited for gangs.

13

Post-Code Red Alliance

After codered ends, only remaining members of gangs can do alliance to warn LSPD to leave that area.

14

LSPD Search Protocol

LSPD can only search if they eliminate all the parties fighting in that zone.

15

Zone Boundary Combat

Gangs must fight within the boundary of zone - killing out of zone will be considered as RDM.

RDM Penalties Apply

Critical EMS Restriction

EMS is not allowed to respond at TURF zones in any condition, including within 500M of TURF Zone area. Gang members are also not allowed to call EMS in this case.

LSPD is allowed to respond at Turf Zones but must put dispatch before responding on that specific zone.

Ban Duration for Violations: 2 Days

Territory Zone Rules

Comprehensive regulations for spray zones, influence areas, and territory conflicts including specific combat protocols.

1

Zone Overlay Monitoring

Stay within the zone by keeping an eye on the left-side overlay; if it's not visible, you're out of the action.

21

Vehicle Spraying Prohibition

You can only spray at territories while on foot - spraying while riding a vehicle is bug abuse

Permanent Ban

Critical Violations & Penalties

Permanent Bans

Vehicle spraying and bug abuse result in permanent bans with no appeals

48-Hour Bans

Kidnapping from territories and prohibited phrases

Menu Suspension

Family members not in zone influence after 3 warnings
